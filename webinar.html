Frequently Asked Questions

How can I sign up for a webinar?

Click on the "Register Now" button for your chosen webinar, and follow the prompts.

Are these webinars live or recorded?

All webinars are live, but recordings will be available afterward for registered users.

Do I need to pay to attend webinars?

Most webinars are free. Some premium sessions may have a small fee, which will be mentioned in the webinar details.

How do I access webinar recordings?

Once the webinar is over, recordings can be accessed under your dashboard if you're logged in.
