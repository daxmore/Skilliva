What people ask us most?

To find freelance projects on Skilliva, start by creating a profile highlighting your skills and experience. Use the search feature to browse available projects that match your expertise. You can also set up alerts to be notified when new projects are posted in your area of interest.

Visit the Skilliva website and look for a "Sign Up" or "Register" button, usually at the top of the homepage. Click on it, and you'll be prompted to enter your details such as email, name, and a password. After filling in the required information, follow the instructions to verify your account and complete the setup.

To improve your chances of getting hired on Skilliva, ensure your profile is complete with a professional photo, detailed work history, and relevant skills. Tailor your proposals to the specific needs of each project, showcase your past work through a portfolio, and gather positive reviews from clients to build credibility.

If you're looking to post a project, log into your Skilliva account and navigate to the "Post a Project" section. Fill out the project details including the scope of work, budget, deadlines, and any specific requirements. Review the information and publish the project to make it visible to freelancers.

Payments on Skilliva are typically managed through an escrow system. When a project is awarded, the client deposits the agreed amount into escrow. Once the freelancer completes the project and the client approves the work, the funds are released from escrow to the freelancer. Payment methods may include bank transfer, PayPal, or other supported payment gateways.
